The candle incident

In 1997, when VIAFIT was just starting to get popularity, a candle set ablaze almost all of the records of the members and staff, thus risking the whole business, also known as "the candle incident".

This set us on a journey to do things the right way.

Professionalism.

How we started

We started out 30 years ago, when the father of mr. Bob Sixpack had nothing more than a dream, hard work, and a few million dollars. We said to ourselves that size doesn't matter, so we started out small, having few members, but loyal. It worked great, but now, we want to go to the next level. We want to go Big.
